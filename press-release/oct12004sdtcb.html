Eclipse Wins Big at SD Readers' Choice Awards

October 1, 2004 - The Eclipse Foundation is pleased to announce it was the major award winner at the recent SD Readers' Choice Awards. Eclipse received awards for Best Value, Best Open Source Tool and Most Improved Tool.

"Winning these awards is a confirmation of the excellent technology and continued innovation Eclipse is providing to the IT community," explains Mike Milinkovich, Executive Director of Eclipse Foundation. "Congratulations to all the great open source developers that contribute countless hours to making Eclipse the success it is."
