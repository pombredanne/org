Borland becomes a Strategic Developer
Member of the Eclipse Foundation


Borland has become a Strategic Developer Member of the Eclipse Foundation. Raaj Shinde, vice president of product strategy and Architecture at Borland will take a seat on the Eclipse Board of Directors. Borland has been an Eclipse member since the inception of Eclipse.
