WebGain and eclipse.org

WebGain helps enterprises accelerate their application development by providing end-to-end development solutions. WebGain's TopLink data integration product line is used by Fortune 500 companies to accelerate and simplify the integration of Java and web applications with their corporate databases and legacy applications. It is one of the leading data integration products on the market. We are pleased that Eclipse.org has asked WebGain to participate in the Eclipse initiative to help drive open standards for data integration.
