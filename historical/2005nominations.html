eclipse board election


As defined by the Bylaws of the Eclipse Foundation, each year elections are held for representatives of two groups within the Eclipse community:

  • Add-in Providers: Each year, Board members are elected to represent the interests of the Add-In Provider class of Members. Each Add-In Provider Member receives one vote.

  • Committers: Each year, Board members are elected to represent the interests of the Committers working on Eclipse projects. Each Committer receives one vote. However, all of the committers who work for a single company have their votes combined to comprise a single vote.

key dates

The key dates for the Add-in Provider representative elections are:

  • January 24, 2005: List of nominees and their personal pages made available on www.eclipse.org. Discussion of the issues on the eclipse.foundation newsgroup will be encouraged.
  • February 7, 2005: Voting begins.
  • February 18, 2005: Voting ends.
  • February 28, 2005: New representatives announced
The key dates for the Committer representative elections have been extended and are:
  • February 7-11, 2005: Nominations to be sent by email to emo at eclipse.org
  • February 16-18, 2005: List of new nominees and their personal pages made available on www.eclipse.org. Discussion of the issues on the eclipse.foundation newsgroup will be encouraged.
  • February 21, 2005: Voting begins.
  • February 25, 2005: Voting ends.
  • February 28, 2005: New representatives announced
